YRJCite Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to YRJCite! Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when using our service. YRJCite is a tool designed to help users create citations in various formats such as IEEE, BibTex, and MLA. We aim to make the academic process easier while maintaining a strong commitment to your privacy.

Permissions

YRJCite requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: Allows YRJCite to access the current active tab to generate citations for the webpage you're viewing. This permission is required to detect the URL and gather necessary information to create a citation for the page.
  • storage: Allows YRJCite to store your preferences and settings, ensuring a seamless experience across sessions. This is necessary to remember your citation format choices and other preferences for future use.
  • downloads: Allows YRJCite to facilitate saving generated citations to your local device for future use. This permission ensures you can download and save the citations you generate.
  • remote code use: YRJCite may use remote code to ensure that your generated citations are formatted correctly and to keep the extension up-to-date with the latest changes. This is essential for the functionality of the extension and to provide the best user experience possible.

Data Collection

YRJCite does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not track your browsing activity or share your data with third parties. The data we access is solely used to generate citations for you and is processed locally within the extension itself.

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect through the permissions listed above is used to enhance your experience with YRJCite. For example, the storage permission ensures your citation preferences are saved between sessions. The downloads permission allows you to save your citation outputs locally on your device. The activeTab permission is essential for gathering details from the current webpage to generate the citation, and remote code usage ensures that citations are generated accurately.

Compliance with Developer Program Policies

To ensure compliance with Google’s Developer Program Policies, we certify that the data usage of YRJCite adheres to the guidelines. By using YRJCite, you agree that your data usage complies with these policies. This certification can be confirmed on the Privacy Practices tab of the item edit page.

Manifest Version Compliance

Please note that new extensions must be developed using Manifest V3. YRJCite complies with this requirement, as Manifest V2 extensions are no longer supported for publication.

Single Purpose Description

YRJCite is a single-purpose extension designed solely to generate citations in various formats (IEEE, BibTex, MLA) from web pages and documents. No other data is accessed or used beyond this core functionality.

Screenshots and Video

As part of our extension publication process, we provide screenshots or videos to demonstrate the functionality of YRJCite. These media showcase the ease of use and practical features of the extension for citation management.

Security

Your privacy is important to us. YRJCite does not store any personal data on our servers. All data generated is stored locally on your device, and we take necessary precautions to ensure that your data remains secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here, with an updated date at the top of this page.
