YRJCite Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to YRJCite! Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when using our service.

Permissions

YRJCite requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: Allows YRJCite to access the current active tab to generate citations for the webpage you're viewing.
  • storage: Allows YRJCite to store your preferences and settings, ensuring a seamless experience across sessions.
  • downloads: Allows YRJCite to facilitate saving generated citations to your local device for future use.

Data Collection

YRJCite does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not track your browsing activity or share your data with third parties.

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to enhance your experience with YRJCite. For example, the storage permission ensures your citation preferences are saved between sessions. The downloads permission allows you to save your citation outputs locally.

Security

Your privacy is important to us. YRJCite does not store any personal data on our servers. All data generated is stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here, with an updated date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at shanto@yrjournal.org.
